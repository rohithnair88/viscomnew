Features

eStore3D

    3D Experience

  • Experience sale-items in 3D with operations such as Zoom, Rotate.

    Customize Appearance

  • Customize Sofas, Chairs with different colors and textures of materials such as cloth, leather etc.

    Light controls

  • Experience lamps with controls for on/off, intensity and color.

    Animation

  • Tryout moving parts such as “cabinet doors” with 3D animation.

    InScene & Standalone

  • Visualize sale-items individually and in context i.e InScene.

    Download

  • Download 3D models of sale-items and insert into eSpace3D App.
  • Enables users to “design” their spaces from the comfort of their own home.
  • Users experience greater satisfaction and lower cost.

eSpace3D App

    Scale accurate 3D model

  • Shoot pictures of your space using an ordinary smartphone and upload to eSpace3D App.
  • A scale-accurate 3D model will be reconstructed using state of the art AI & 3D technology.
  • A “digital twin” of your space will be provided to enable a try-n-buy experience in designing your space.
  • It could be “Living Room” or a “Dentist Office”.

    Furniture

  • Scale accurate 3D models of Sofas, Chairs, Tables, Lamps can be downloaded from eStore3D.
  • Downloaded models can be inserted into eSpace3D App. Design your space to your liking.

    Paints, Tiles

  • Palettes of Paints and Tiles can be downloaded from eStore3D.
  • Apply to Walls, Floors etc.

    Lighting controls

  • Download lamps from eStore3D and insert into eSpace3D App.
  • Control lights via On/Off Switch & Intensity to your liking.

    Save / Open

  • You can save the state of eSpace3D App into any file and re-open later.

    Transforms

  • Move, Rotate & Scale any object (e.g Sofa).

    Scene Controls

  • Zoom, Pan, Rotate the overall Scene.

    Undo / Redo
