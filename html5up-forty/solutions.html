Features

3D Experience

  • Experience sale items in Full 3D
  • Zoom, Rotate objects
  • View objects in various environments such as a beach or living room.

Change Appearance

  • Change color of sofa cloth
  • Change textures
  • Tryout various types of legs e.g Stainless steel, Wood

Light Controls

  • Turn lights on / off
  • Change color of light with controls for Red, Green, Blue
  • Change Brightness

Operate Cabinets

  • Open and close doors
  • Turn on lights inside

Dimensions

  • View length, width and other dimensions
  • Use centi-meters, meters or feet

Standalone & InScene

  • View sale-items in Standalone and Inscene mode.
  • Inscene mode allows combinations such as lamps, lights, paints.
